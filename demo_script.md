# TaskGenie - Hackathon Demo Script (3 Mins)

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
**Visual**: Show TaskGenie Dashboard (Empty or minimal tasks).
**Voiceover**: "Hi judges! This is TaskGenie, a productivity tool that cares about your *vibe*, not just your output. Built with Gemini 1.5 Flash, it’s designed to prevent burnout before it starts."

## Scene 2: Voice Command & Structured Data (0:30 - 1:15)
**Action**: Click microphone / Type command.
**Voice Input**: "Please remind me to finish the Physics Lab Report by Friday night, it's super important!"
**Visual**: Show task appearing instantly with:
    -   Priority: High (Inferred from "super important")
    -   Due Date: Next Friday (Calculated by Gemini)
    -   Category: Education
**Voiceover**: "Notice how Gemini 1.5 Flash instantly parses my chaotic request into structured data, setting the correct priority and deadline without me even clicking a dropdown."

## Scene 3: The Burnout Shield (1:15 - 2:00)
**Action**: Add 3-4 more high-priority tasks with "Today" as due date.
**Action**: Click "Get AI Insights" (or refresh if auto).
**Visual**: A warning or advice box appears: "⚠️ High Burnout Risk! You have 4 critical tasks due today. Suggestion: Tackle the Physics Lab first for 45 mins, then take a 15-min walk."
**Voiceover**: "But here's the magic. TaskGenie analyzes my *entire* workload. It sees I'm overloaded and warns me, offering a specific 'Vibe Restoration' strategy. It's like a coach, not a list."

## Scene 4: Gamification (2:00 - 2:40)
**Action**: Complete a task.
**Visual**: XP Bar goes up! "Rank Up: Private First Class!"
**Voiceover**: "And to keep me motivated, I earn XP for every task. It turns productivity into a game."

## Scene 5: Conclusion (2:40 - 3:00)
**Visual**: Show "About" page with Gemini Badge.
**Voiceover**: "TaskGenie: Powered by Gemini 1.5 Flash, built with Node.js. Thanks for watching!"
